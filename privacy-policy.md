# Privacy Policy for Reese’s Maths

**Last updated: 09 December 2025**

Reese’s Maths ("the App") is developed by Andrew Gray ("the Developer").  
This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Reese’s Maths **does not collect, store, share, or process any personal data**.

The App does **not** collect:

- Names or contact information  
- Location data  
- Device identifiers  
- Usage analytics or diagnostic data  
- Financial information  
- Photos, videos, or audio  
- Any other personal information  

All activity stays on the user’s device, and the App operates entirely offline.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App uses **no third-party services or SDKs** that collect data, including:

- No Google Analytics  
- No advertising SDKs  
- No social media integrations  
- No cloud services or external servers  

The App does not transmit any data outside the device.

---

## 3. Children’s Privacy

Reese’s Maths is designed for children.  
Because the App collects **no data**, no personal information from children is ever stored or transmitted.

---

## 4. Data Storage

Since the App collects no data, no personal or usage information is stored, retained, or backed up by the Developer.

---

## 5. Security

As no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no user data that could be exposed or accessed.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

If the App is updated in a way that introduces data collection or processing, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Users will be notified through the Google Play Store listing.
